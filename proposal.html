Info 340 Project Proposal

Alexandre Labbe, Shawn Leonard, Jaiden York, Carson Klein

Problem Domain

Our group is interested in where the different bike racks on campus are. Many people bike to campus and need places to park their bikes. We have decided to focus on the lack of public information about the different locations of bike racks on campus. While a seasoned campus-biker may know where their favorite places to park, somebody who is new to campus may not know where to park their bike. For example, the first time Alex biked to campus, he followed google maps to a lecture hall on the quad, but there was no bike rack nearby. If he had been able to have an application that would have showed him where the bike racks on campus were, he would have just had google maps route him to the bike rack closest to his lecture hall. Similar solutions have been done before, such as a map of every bike rack in downtown seattle, populated by information from a dataset of the bike rack locations, and information about them. There have also been other applications that allow users to upload bike rack locations. But nothing of the sort has been done for the UW campus. We hope to be the ones to solve this problem in a manner that is catered for huskies.
